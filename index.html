Package Component Definitions

  • Essential/Team: Entry-level plan with core features.
  • Professional/Business: Includes advanced analytics and security.
  • Enterprise: Includes SSO, data-residency, and large-scale support.
  • Total Minutes: Monthly included minutes.
  • Estimated Calls: Based on an average of four minutes per call.
  • Price: What the client pays monthly.
  • Internal Cost: Cost of delivering service (LLM + telco).
  • Overage PPM: Per-minute charge after plan limit.

Business Plan Overview

What: AI-powered voice agents for automating customer service and sales.
How: Twilio-based platform, real-time voice AI in twenty-six languages, analytics, and concurrency.
Who: Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), primarily in the U.S. food and beverage sector.

Problem & Solution

Problem: Staffing issues, call volume spikes, high costs.
Solution: Multilingual, automated AI agents with 24/7 availability and analytics.
